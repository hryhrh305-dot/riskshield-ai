## Message hierarchy

1. **Category:** second-line pre-send risk governance.
2. **Outcome:** approve high-value campaigns before the first send.
3. **Mechanism:** available signals become Send, Review, and Suppress decisions.
4. **Evidence:** reasons, explicit unknowns, aligned surfaces, and exportable audit records.
5. **Boundary:** decision support, not an inbox, delivery, revenue, or fraud guarantee.
