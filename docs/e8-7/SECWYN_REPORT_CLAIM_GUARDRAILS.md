# Secwyn E8.7 Report Claim Guardrails

## Allowed

- Campaign Contact Risk Audit
- Pre-Send Risk Audit
- Send / Review / Suppress distribution
- Input rows, rejected rows, duplicates, unique processed and results produced
- Required Actions derived from recorded result actions
- Top Risk Drivers derived from negative primary reasons
- Evidence available at the time of audit
- Domain evidence, mailbox evidence, unknown, not-tested and lookup-failed states
- Viewing, downloading and printing do not consume additional contact Credits

## Requires qualification

- **Send:** suitable for controlled campaign use under current evidence, not guaranteed delivery or inbox placement.
- **Mailbox confirmed:** only when current recorded mailbox evidence is actually `confirmed`; still not inbox proof.
- **Client-ready:** professionally structured Secwyn report, not white-label, human-reviewed, certified or insured.
- **Audit metadata:** only versions, IDs and times actually present in the result rows.
- **Partial report:** must state the mismatch and must not appear complete.

## Forbidden in active report/runtime copy

- Compliance Certificate, Safety Certificate, Guaranteed Deliverability Report
- Guaranteed inbox, zero bounce, safe to send, revenue preserved, domains protected
- Potential savings, waste prevented, bounces prevented or ROI as achieved outcomes
- Underwritten, insured, liability-free, approved by, human-reviewed
- Invented client, campaign, reviewer, owner, workspace, SLA or white-label identity
- Spam-trap, fraud, malicious, synthetic-identity or cross-site behavior claims without direct evidence and an approved future capability
- “AI” for deterministic report text

## Static sample boundary

The E8.6 homepage sample remains illustrative and must continue to say it is not a real customer report. E8.7 does not use that fixture as an actual audit or proof of customer outcomes.
